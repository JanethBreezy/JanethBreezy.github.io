A summary of the Essex Market history(Based on the Essex Market Website)

The Essex Market used to be a hub for independent pushcart peddlers. The people in those pushcarts sold a lot of vegetables and other such that people started to buy off from them. However it got extremely overcrowded in that particular area that they had to create a public indoor market, such as the Essex Market. Soon, the Puerto Rican immigrants arrived to the Lower East Side due to the new migration wave. As time goes by many years later,the Essex Market became a development project. Which became a success! To this day you can visit the market in the Lower East Side.
